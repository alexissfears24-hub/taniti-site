Getting to and around Taniti

Most visitors arrive by air. Some arrive via a weekly cruise stop at Yellow Leaf Bay. Choose the best way to move based on your plans.

Arriving

Air

Small airport supports small jets and prop planes. Expansion is underway for larger jets in future years.

Cruise

Small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week.
